What Makes a Rainbow?

A rainbow is an arch of colours visible in the sky, caused by the refraction and dispersion of the sun's light by rain or other water droolets in atmosphere. So to see a rainbow, you need mixed weather with a bit of sunshine and a bit of rain too!
